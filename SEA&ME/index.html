What is SEA&ME?

Company Culture

Founded in 2021, SEA&ME has become one of the most popular hot pot restaurants. We always uphold the idea of “putting customers first and providing them with the best services”, and change traditional, standardized and single services into personalized services through innovation.

Our mission

We devote ourselves to creating happy hot pot time and spreading healthy hot pot culture to foodies all over the world through selected products and innovative services. One person per table, with smaller pots. No disturbance from others. You do not have to eat twice as much as you can!

Staff character

Our staff boast qualities of honesty, innovation, modesty, diligence and passion. They are kind to people and have a sense of responsibility.

Connotation

We advocate equality and grant power to our staff; we make progress through continuous learning and innovation; we always reflect on ourselves and think twice before taking actions; we are honest, trustworthy and responsible; we are kind to everyone and grateful to our customers; we have great passion and encourage teamwork.
