Boil whole chicken, scallion, ginger in simmer for 2 hours.
Mix Sichuan pepper, fennel, star anise, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cumin, black cardamon and clove.
Pour in cooking wine. Soak 30 minutes.
Boil dry chili peppers for 5 minutes and cut it into pieces.
Boil the material above together with garlic and bean paste as sauce.
Cut the meat and add salt and eggs.
Cut the shrimps into fragments and add egg white.

Prepare the soup base from the sauce and the chicken soup.
Put in meat, shrimps, vegetables and so on.

Enjoy the hot pot.
