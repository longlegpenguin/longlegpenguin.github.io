meat
# Dish Price(FT/100g)
1 Fatty Beef Slice

It is made of chuck steaks in the 1-6 ribs on selected cattle that have been imported and naturally fed for more than 100 days with grain. Each cattle only carry 20 kg of them. After procedures of ageing, dividing into natural blocks and slicing into thin slices, the taste becomes more delicate and smoother.

HUF 1699
2 Spicy Marinated Beef

It is made of topside and oyster blade, which are the tenderest parts of cattle' hind legs and fore legs. The shape is like a cucumber, commonly known as cucumber strip. After 9 complicated procedures such as defrosting, tearing fascia, dividing, slicing and marinating, the taste becomes smooth and tender but not tough, which makes it the most popular dish in Haidilao.

HUF 1699
3 Hot Pot Steak

It is made of tender meat in the centre of chuck steak, which is called top blade steak or oyster blade. Each cattle only have 3 kilograms of them. After several complicated procedures such as defrosting, tearing fascia, dividing, slicing and marinating, the taste becomes tender and chewy with a meaty flavour.

HUF 1699
4 Juicy Beef Meatball

It is made of beef rumps in cattle' hind legs, treated with procedures of aging, smashing, whipping into smashed beef pastes, kneading into meatballs and wrapping soup jelly which is made from old chicken and ham. After the soup boils and meatball floats, cook for about 3 minutes and serve. When eating with dipping sauce for fish ball and shrimp paste, it presents us a more prominent flavour. The temperature of juicy beef meatballs' central soup is high, be careful when eat them.
3db

HUF 999
5 Grass Fed Lamb Slice

It is made of lambs of no more than 12 months old which are from Xilingol, Hulunbeir and other grasslands of Inner Mongolia. After procedures of aging, slitting and freezing, the taste becomes tender and juicy, without the smell of mutton. After the soup boils, cook for about 30 seconds and serve.

HUF 2999
6 Beef Tripe

It is made of beef tripes, treated with procedures of washing, tearing into small pieces, slitting, and mixture with papain. After the soup boils, cook for 15 seconds (dip 8 times and take out 7 times) and serve. It tastes crispy and tender.

HUF 1299
7 Quail Egg

Carefully selected, the shiny quail eggs taste smooth, delicate and mellow.
